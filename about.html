Today Al Alfi family continues its developmental work through Al Alfi Foundation which focuses on promoting education

Founded by Moataz Al Alfi, a prominent Egyptian businessman and a visionary philanthropist, the foundation is a celebration and continuation of the family legacy to invest in developing human capital through education and capacity building, particularly among Egyptian scientists, educators and gifted students in STEM.

the Founder’s messge

Philanthropy, my devotion to improve human welfare, has always been a main factor of my identity; which has been encouraged through my culture, religion and foremost my family moralities.

To care for those less fortunate is a principle in all monotheistic religions and one of the most valuable teachings of Islam expressed in the Quran Verse in Surat Al Baqara: “261: “The example of those who spend their wealth in the way of Allah is like a seed [of grain] which grows seven spikes; in each spike is a hundred grains.

Mission

AFF aims to attain the continuity of Al Alfi family legacy of serving the community by fostering, empowering and enabling the environment that leads to a knowledge-based economy

Vision

AFF Vision is to become the recognized civil society institution contributing to the establishment of a national framework for STEM education, talent discovery and talent development to pave the way towards the sought

Our Strategic Objectives

To advocate and facilitate the development of a National Strategy for STEM Education. Raise the Public Awareness of STEM Education, its value, its importance and its impact on the Economy. Advance the position of Egypt on the scale of the Math and Science indicators. Focus on capacity building efforts in research and innovation to serve the national challenges.

Acquire, adopt and implement the best practices in Talent discovery and talent development in STEM.
